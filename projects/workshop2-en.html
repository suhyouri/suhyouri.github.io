Memory Circuit Workshop

Workshop · PSO(Pizza Savor Office) · 2025

A workshop where participants create circuits that make memories. Participants learn the basics of electronic circuits and build circuits that record 1 bit.


Target Audience:
People curious about what Memory Circuits are
People curious about the materials that create computer memory
People curious about electricity and electronic circuits
People who want to discuss these topics
