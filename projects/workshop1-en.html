Inanimate Techno Party Workshop

Workshop · 2024 · Arts Management Support Center · Super Testbed

DELIRIOUS HOME🏡 started from the idea: 'What if familiar objects had minds of their own and moved?' Through the walking mirror🚶🏻‍♀️, emotional fan🫨, bean-picking chopsticks🥢, and inanimate techno🪴, I attempted to visualize living consciousness and replicate their movements—'approaching and peeking,' 'placating,' 'inevitably failing,' and 'dancing.
