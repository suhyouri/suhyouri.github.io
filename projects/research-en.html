Research on the Walking Methods of Inanimate Objects

Ongoing Research · 2025 · Seongnam Cultural Foundation — Young Artist Support Grant

An ongoing research project investigating how inanimate objects move—or appear to move—and what that motion implies about presence and aliveness.

This research explores the various ways non-living things traverse space and the philosophical implications of their movement. How do we perceive motion in objects we know to be lifeless? What does their "walking" tell us about our understanding of life itself?
