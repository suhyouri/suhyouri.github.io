WMLAlive: The Body as an Island

Solo Exhibition · 2023 · spaceuooyoung · Seoul Foundation for Arts and Culture (SFAC)

An ongoing project exploring the body, presence, and the question of aliveness. WMLAlive emerges from a fascination with the moments we misjudge life—what we thought was alive, dead; what we thought was dead, alive.

The project investigates the criteria and processes behind these judgments. At the boundary between the living and the non-living, I ask: on what basis do we consider "that body" to be alive? Or not alive?

Through the ghostliness of the inanimate, I continue my attempt to speak about "the body as evidence of existence" and what it means to be alive.
