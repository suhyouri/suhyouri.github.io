Circuit Making Memories — PCB Kit

2024 · Gyeonggi Cultural Foundation — Artist Self-Reliance Support

A DIY PCB kit consisting of memory-making circuits (A–C), an oscillator (D), and a continuity test (E) — exploring memory and electronic circuits as artistic materials. By building memory-making circuits, participants examine how computers remember and forget. Through exploring the components of memory together, and via the design and assembly of printed circuit boards, participants investigate the relationship between electronic pathways and the pathways of memory.

  • A. Materials of Memory
    A board for creating the logic circuits NOT, AND, and OR—the materials that constitute computer memory.
  • B. Latch (Circuit Making Memories)
    A board that creates a circuit to remember and forget 1 bit using NOT, AND, and OR logic gates.
  • C. Memory Circuit (A+B)
    A workshop board that combines (A) Materials of Memory and (B) Latch circuit.
  • D. OSC
    An oscillator board that produces sawtooth waves, allowing experimentation with various sounds by changing capacitor and resistor values.
  • E. Mini Handshake
    A board for testing electrical connections by flowing microcurrent.
