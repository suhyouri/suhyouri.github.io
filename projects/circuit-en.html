Circuit Making Memories — PCB Kit

2024 · Gyeonggi Cultural Foundation — Artist Self-Reliance Support

A DIY PCB kit exploring memory and electronic circuit as artistic material. This project examines how circuits can embody and create memories.

Through the design and assembly of custom printed circuit boards, participants explore the relationship between electronic pathways and the pathways of memory. The kit invites users to think about technology not just as a tool, but as a medium capable of holding and expressing personal narratives.
