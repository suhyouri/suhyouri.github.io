✦ Youri Suh

✦ 서유리

Korean visual artist based in Seoul, working with play and electronic circuits. Since 2023, running WMLAlive (What Makes Life Alive), exploring the body and presence. Currently pursuing M.A. in Multimedia Film, Korea National University of Arts (K-Arts) → CV.
